Description

A classic pizza typically consists of a homemade or store-bought dough base, topped with tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, and various toppings such as pepperoni, mushrooms, and bell peppers. It is often baked in a wood-fired or gas oven until the crust is crispy and the cheese is melted. Some variations may also include additional ingredients such as olives, anchovies, or even pineapple. A classic pizza can be served either in slices or as a whole pie, and is often considered a staple dish in Italian cuisine.
