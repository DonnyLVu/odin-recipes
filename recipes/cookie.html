Description

A chocolate chip cookie is a type of drop cookie made from flour, sugar, butter, eggs, vanilla extract and chocolate chips. The dough is formed into small balls, then baked on a baking sheet until golden brown. The chocolate chips can be semi-sweet, milk or dark chocolate. They can be soft or crispy, depending on the recipe and baking time. Chocolate chip cookies are a popular treat and are often served as a dessert or snack. They can also be made with different variations like nuts, oats, or different types of chocolate.
